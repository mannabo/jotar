About JOTAR

JOTAR is a non-governmental organization dedicated to transforming agricultural communities through innovative programs, sustainable practices, and community empowerment.

We believe in the power of education, technology, and collaboration to create lasting positive change in the agricultural sector.

Our Programs & Initiatives

Modern Farming Training

Organizing specialized workshops and training sessions focusing on the use of IoT, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and farm automation technologies. This program aims to equip members with practical knowledge and hands-on skills to increase efficiency, productivity, and competitiveness in modern agriculture.

Young Agropreneurs

A mentor-mentee program designed to connect young agropreneurs with experienced industry experts. The initiative provides guidance, industry insights, and networking opportunities to shape the next generation of competitive and innovative farmers.

Digital Agro Marketing

Empowering members to master digital marketing strategies, including e-commerce, social media campaigns, and branding techniques. This initiative helps agropreneurs expand their product reach, strengthen their brand visibility, and adapt to the digital economy.

JOTAR Green Community

A platform for community engagement and Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives focused on environmental sustainability. Activities include green awareness campaigns, eco-friendly farming practices, and community-based projects that promote long-term sustainability.

Agro Innovation Hub

Building strong collaborations with higher education institutions (HEIs), research bodies, and technology partners to explore and develop cutting-edge agricultural technologies. This hub serves as a driver of innovation, knowledge sharing, and research commercialization for the agropreneur community.
